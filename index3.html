ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

Artificial intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines to perform tasks like learning, problem-solving, and decision-making. It is a rapidly advancing field that uses algorithms to analyze data and improve over time, enabling applications from virtual assistants and self-driving cars to advancements in healthcare and finance. While AI offers benefits like increased speed and accuracy, it also raises concerns regarding job displacement and data privacy, making its responsible development a crucial topic for the future.
